About Double Panda

Double Panda is an exciting platformer that challenges players to control two pandas simultaneously, each with unique abilities. The game is filled with engaging puzzles, obstacles, and enemies that require teamwork and strategic thinking to overcome. Perfect for puzzle enthusiasts and fans of platform games, Double Panda combines fun gameplay with adorable characters, making it a delightful experience for players of all ages.

How To Play Double Panda

Panda 1 Controls

Use Arrow Keys to move

Panda 2 Controls

Use WASD Keys to move

Action Controls

  • Spacebar: Action/Fire
  • Enter: Start/Pause
  • Esc: Open/Close menu
  • R: Restart level
